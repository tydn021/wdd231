History of Storybook Style!

Back in the early days of Hollywood, California, movie sets were a huge undertaking. Many fairytale and medieval themed films were shot at this time, and so each set had to be handcrafted from scratch. The builders of these movie sets however would often fall in love with the sets they've created. Many of these workers would go on to build their own houses in the style of whimsical fairy-tale like European architecture.
