Hugh Comstock

  • Birth: April 17th, 1893
  • Death: June 1st, 1950

If you read through the information on the houses presented on this site, you will notice a name come up repeatedly; Hugh Comstock. A man from humble origins being born in Evanston, Illinois on a farm, his family would sell the farm and move to California. Hugh Comstock would eventually find himself serving in Word War 1 and returning to start a life with his wife
